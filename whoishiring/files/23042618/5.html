We call this peeling the news for perspective from the past. We peel the news by pointing out the history behind it with a brief analysis in weekday peels and extensive analysis in weekend perspectives posted on our site and social media and delivered to our subscribers' inboxes.

The Opportunity: ThePeel.news has a social marketing opportunity for a candidate with expertise in social media engagement and analysis. It's helpful, but not necessary, for this candidate to also have a passion for news analysis and enjoy learning about the historical context of current events.

This is an unpaid, volunteer part-time position. The work will be conducted remotely.
